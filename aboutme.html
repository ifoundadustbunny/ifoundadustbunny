About Me

Hello! I'm Cora. I'm an artist and I love to work on music, paintings, jewelry, and other arts and crafts. I go to school to learn about the outdoors and I can frequently be found in the outdoors. Sometimes I want to ride my bicycle.

I created this site in a daze while throwing together frankensteined code from a very similar project I abandoned a couple of years ago. Recently, I've had the pleasure to see a lot of friends and loved ones going back to email lists and old tech in an effort to move away from The Horrors of it all, and it inspired me to create this site.

Hope you enjoy clicking around!
